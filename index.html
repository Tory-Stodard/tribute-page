Sir Theodorius Maximus

The weiner dog that saved a million lives.

  • Sir Theodorius Maximus, or Theo for short, was the fastest weiner alive. The legend goes that he could outrun a cheteah without even trying.
  • Just his bark alone were scare off any predators in the nearby area. He was feared by all. And for good reason. It was said the he could rip up a chew toy in as little as 5 minutes. His mamma stoped buying him chew toys because they wouldn't last enough.
  • Despite his fearsome attitude, he was always professional. He was never seen without his tie.
  • How did he save a million lives you might ask? There once was an evil rooster that lived down the road. The rooster was know to too terrorize the neightboord by waking everyone up with his cockadoodledoo's every morning. Sir Theodorius Maximus put an end to it... by barking at him.
